B
Special Symbols

The Racket language supports the usage of Unicode characters in both strings and identifiers. These symbols may be entered directly into DrRacket by typing the codes shown in Tables B-1, B-2, B-3, and B-4. Once the code is entered, hold down the A L T key immediately followed by the \ key (this works in Windows and Linux, but for macOS use CTL-\ instead).
