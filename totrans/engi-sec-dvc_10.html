ADVANCED DEVICE SECURITY CONCEPTS

Many managers and developers dream of one-shot security solutions. They believe that a secure device results from just activating a protection feature in the OS kernel or by just integrating a security component into the PCB design of the product. The third part of this book covers concepts that can have a strong impact on device security, like system integrity, reliable update procedures, and robustness even under attack.
