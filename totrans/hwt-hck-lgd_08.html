6 Busting In and Getting Busted!

While the solution at the end of Chapter 5 does provide a working bypass for AppLocker and Constrained Language mode, it’s simply not practical to rebuild a project every time we launch a single command. To address that, we’ll crank up our previous build code to include a PowerShell-like console that interactively executes commands and displays the output. We can then use that to execute the PowerView script along with other reconnaissance commands and gather a bunch of new information.
