About the Author

John Boxall has been an electronics enthusiast for more than 30 years, spending much of that time in the electronics retail, wholesale, and consulting fields. He also spent several years writing Arduino tutorials, projects, and reviews of kits and accessories at https://tronixstuff.com. His other books include Arduino Workshop, 2nd edition (2021), and AVR Workshop (2022), both from No Starch Press.
