MAKING AND USING CONTAINERS

Containers are essential to modern application architecture. They simplify packaging, deploying, and scaling application components. They enable building reliable and resilient applications that handle failure gracefully. However, containers can also be confusing. They look like completely different systems, with separate hostnames, networking, and storage, but they do not have many of the features of a separate system, such as a separate console or system services.
