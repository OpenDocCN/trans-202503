4 Perfecting the Hook

It’s time to craft our perfect phishing email and gather a list of potential victims. First, we need a convincing domain for our email to come from. We know that a freshly registered domain is a red flag for anti-spam tools. If we can’t register a new domain for fear of having it flagged as suspicious by spam filters, how can we acquire one? A viable solution is to simply recycle an old domain.
